# Backlog Staging Requirements

## Purpose

The initial backlog must stage work in product slices that are independently understandable and reviewable.

## Requirements

1. The first staged slice must establish the local operating model and service kernel baseline.
2. The second staged slice must cover scenario loading, simulation control, and deterministic replay.
3. The third staged slice must cover platform bootstrap and GitOps deployment.
4. The fourth staged slice must cover conformance testing and a drop-in replacement proof.
5. Each staged slice must map back to at least one feature brief under `docs/features/`.
6. Backlog items should be grouped by stage tracks rather than by low-level tool categories alone.
